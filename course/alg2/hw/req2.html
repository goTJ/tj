Longest increasing subsequence (LIS)

The longest increasing subsequence is to find a longest increasing subsequence of a given sequence of distinct integers a1,a2…an .




You should complete the program and be saved as "ID.cpp" in one file, i.e. if your ID is b92902001, then your file should be b92922001.cpp or  b92922001.c
TA will examine your code so you have to show the answers :  1. the length of LIS
                                                                                                   2. the biggest three integers of LIS
                                                                                                   3. the smallest three integers of LIS
                                                                                                   4. total execution time ( include all your code)

                                                                                                  
The sequence input information is in input.txt with the following format:
-----------------
9
2
5
3
7
11
8
10
-----------------
Each line shows distinct integers a1,a2…an .
